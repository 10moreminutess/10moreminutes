Our Community Purpose

As a Community Interest Company, 10 More Minutes exists to benefit people of all ages experiencing poor mental health, loneliness, or emotional distress. We go beyond providing a mental health app—we're building a community platform where people can come together to support one another, reduce isolation, and foster genuine human connection.

Building Sustainable Peer Networks

Our service offers quick, almost instant support in short and manageable conversations: ten minutes at a time, giving people a safe space to be heard when they need it most. Over time, we create sustainable peer-to-peer support networks that empower individuals to both give and receive help, linking people with similar experiences to build genuine, empathetic, and deeply impactful understanding.

How We Create Community Benefit

⚡

Immediate Access

The community benefits by having immediate access to short, manageable emotional support sessions, reducing feelings of isolation at the moment they need it most.

🤝

Authentic Connections

We match people with similar experiences to create authentic connections rooted in shared understanding, reducing stigma and increasing feelings of belonging.

🏥

Professional Pathways

The community benefits by ensuring individuals are guided to the right level of professional care when peer support alone is not enough, improving safety and wellbeing outcomes.

🏢

Workplace Integration

We help organizations and community groups embed 10 More Minutes into their wellbeing practices as first-line mental health support, normalizing conversations and reducing stigma.

🔄

Mutual Support

Users can both give and receive support, fostering empathy, mutual understanding, and resilience while feeling valued whether seeking or providing help.

📈

Continuous Improvement

The community benefits by having a platform that evolves to meet their needs through user feedback, ensuring it remains relevant, effective, and user-led.

About 10 More Minutes Community Interest Company

10 More Minutes is a Community Interest Company (CIC) registered in England. As a CIC, we are legally required to use our profits and assets for community benefit. Any surplus we generate is reinvested directly into developing and delivering our service to further our community purpose.

This includes maintaining and improving our platform, expanding access, providing training for volunteers, conducting outreach to workplaces and communities, and ensuring long-term sustainability of peer-to-peer mental health support.
